— Equipment & Services

Services

Personal workshop equipped with prosumer-grade scanning and printing hardware. Below — the actual tools behind every project, and what each one delivers.

01
Shining 3D Einstar — Handheld Scanning
Handheld structured-light scanner with 0.1 mm precision and built-in color capture. Built for shiny, dark, and reflective car panels — exactly where most scanners fail.
Point distance 0.1 mm 14 FPS scan rate VCSEL infrared Color capture
02
Creality K1 Max — FDM 3D Printing
Enclosed CoreXY printer with 300×300×300 mm build volume. Runs PLA, PETG, ABS, ASA, PA, and PC at up to 600 mm/s. Big enough for splitters and full trim pieces in one shot.
300×300×300 mm 600 mm/s max Hotend ≤300°C PLA · PETG · ABS · ASA · PA · PC
03
Reverse Engineering — Scan to CAD
Raw scan mesh converted into clean parametric CAD geometry. Focused on medium to large automotive body panels and trim pieces — splitters, bumpers, diffusers. No fine-detail or small-part work.
Mesh → CAD Surface reconstruction Print-ready output
04
Complete design and manufacturing service for composite molds
Complete design and manufacturing service for composite molds. The process includes 3D modeling, geometry optimization, production preparation, and 3D printing of functional molds intended for the fabrication of fiberglass, carbon fiber, and other composite components..
Custom aftermarket OEM modification Bolt-on fitment
05
Replication of Broken or Discontinued Parts
Broken, lost, or no longer in production? Scan whatever remains of the original, rebuild it in CAD, print a working replacement. Ideal for older, rare, or out-of-stock parts.
Damaged part recovery Legacy parts CAD rebuild
06
Consultations & Project Planning
Talk through your project before committing. Material, feasibility, approach — honest assessment from a fellow enthusiast.
Project planning Feasibility Material advice
